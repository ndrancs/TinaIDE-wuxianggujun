TinaIDE Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This open-source edition does not provide account login, third-party login, activation codes, membership, or license activation systems.

1. Information We Collect
- Device information: Used for compatibility checks, crash analysis, and app stability improvements.
- Logs and feedback content: Processed only when you explicitly export, upload, or submit feedback.
- Local settings and project data: Stored on your device by default.

2. How We Use Information
- Improve product experience and stability.
- Handle feedback, crashes, and compatibility issues.
- Keep local projects, plugins, download history, and related features working properly.

3. Information Protection
- Local data is stored on your device.
- We do not sell your information.
- Feedback and logs are used only to diagnose issues and improve the product.

4. Your Rights
- Delete local projects, settings, logs, and cached data.
- Disable automatic crash log upload.
- Review and remove sensitive content before submitting feedback.

If you have any questions, contact us through feedback or GitHub Issues.
